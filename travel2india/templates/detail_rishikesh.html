Rishikesh

Yoga capital of the world—where the Ganges meets adventure and spirituality.

Rishikesh, nestled in the Himalayan foothills, is where the Ganges leaves the mountains and enters the plains. It's the global capital of yoga—ashrams line the banks, and meditation retreats draw seekers from around the world. Yet Rishikesh also thrills: white-water rafting on the Ganges, bungee jumping, trekking, and the mesmerizing evening Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat. Whether you seek peace or adrenaline, Rishikesh delivers in the shadow of the Himalayas.

Must-See

  • Evening Ganga Aarti (Triveni Ghat)
  • Lakshman Jhula & Ram Jhula (suspension bridges)
  • White-water rafting (Mar.–Jun.)
  • Yoga & meditation retreats
  • Beatles Ashram (Chaurasi Kutiya)

Best Time to Visit

  • Sep–Nov, Feb–Jun: Pleasant, ideal for yoga & rafting
  • Mar–Jun: Best for rafting (snowmelt)
  • Avoid: Jul–Aug (monsoon, rafting restricted)

Suggested Duration

  • 2–3 days: Aarti, bridges, yoga class, rafting
  • 4–7 days: Yoga retreat, trekking, Haridwar combine
  • Easy add-on from Delhi (5–6 hrs)
