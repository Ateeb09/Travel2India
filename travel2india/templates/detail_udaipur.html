Udaipur

The City of Lakes—Venice of the East, where romance floats on water and palaces rise from the shore.

Udaipur is India's most romantic city—serene lakes, white marble palaces, and narrow streets that invite exploration. The legendary Lake Palace appears to float on Lake Pichola, while City Palace crowns the waterfront with intricate courtyards and mirrored halls. Take a boat ride at sunset, stroll through the old city's bazaars, and witness classical dance and music at Bagore Ki Haveli. Udaipur is where fairy tales feel real.

Must-See

  • City Palace & museum
  • Lake Pichola boat ride
  • Lake Palace (view from boat or dining)
  • Bagore Ki Haveli (cultural show)
  • Jagdish Temple & old city lanes

Best Time to Visit

  • Sep–Mar: Pleasant, ideal for lakes & palaces
  • Oct–Nov: Post-monsoon, lakes full, lush surroundings
  • Avoid: Apr–Jun (very hot, low water levels)

Suggested Duration

  • 2–3 days: City Palace, lake cruise, old city
  • 3–4 days: Add Monsoon Palace, Kumbhalgarh day trip
  • Perfect romantic retreat or Rajasthan finale
