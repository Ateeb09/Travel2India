Mumbai

The City of Dreams—Bollywood, Marine Drive, and the relentless energy of India's financial capital.

Mumbai is where ambition meets the Arabian Sea—a city that never sleeps, home to Bollywood, the Bombay Stock Exchange, and Dabbawalas. From the Gateway of India and Elephanta Caves to the colonial grandeur of Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus and the hip cafes of Bandra, Mumbai blends history, glamour, and street life. Walk the Marine Drive promenade at sunset, sample street food at Chowpatty, and feel the pulse of a city that welcomes dreamers from every corner of India.

Must-See

  • Gateway of India & Elephanta Caves
  • Marine Drive & Chowpatty Beach
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CSMT)
  • Bollywood studios or Film City tour
  • Colaba Causeway, Bandra, Bollywood homes

Best Time to Visit

  • Nov–Feb: Pleasant, ideal for sightseeing
  • Dec–Jan: Cool, minimal humidity
  • Avoid: Apr–Jun (hot, humid), Jun–Sep (heavy monsoon)

Suggested Duration

  • 2–3 days: Gateway, Marine Drive, Elephanta, street food
  • 4–5 days: Add Bollywood tour, beaches, nightlife
  • Hub for Goa, Ajanta-Ellora, Gujarat
