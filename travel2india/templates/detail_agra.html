Agra

Home of the Taj Mahal—the world's most beautiful monument and an eternal symbol of love.

Agra is where Mughal grandeur reaches its zenith. The Taj Mahal needs no introduction—a marble masterpiece that changes hue with the light, from dawn's pink glow to moonlight's silver. Beyond the Taj, explore the mighty Agra Fort, the abandoned city of Fatehpur Sikri, and the tomb of Itmad-ud-Daulah. Walk through lanes where emperors once strode, and feel the weight of history in every stone. Agra is a pilgrimage for lovers of architecture, romance, and India's imperial past.

Must-See

  • Taj Mahal (sunrise or sunset)
  • Agra Fort (Red Fort)
  • Fatehpur Sikri
  • Tomb of Itmad-ud-Daulah
  • Mehtab Bagh (Taj view gardens)

Best Time to Visit

  • Oct–Mar: Best weather for exploring
  • Feb: Pleasant, less fog than winter
  • Avoid: Apr–Jun (scorching heat), Jul–Sep (humid monsoon)

Suggested Duration

  • 1–2 days: Taj + Agra Fort + Fatehpur Sikri
  • 2–3 days: Leisurely pace, Mehtab Bagh sunset
  • Part of Golden Triangle (Delhi–Agra–Jaipur)
