Jaipur

The Pink City—where Rajput pride, royal palaces, and vibrant bazaars create a regal tapestry.

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, dazzles with its pink-hued facades, massive forts, and opulent palaces. From the majestic Amber Fort atop the hills to the delicate honeycomb of Hawa Mahal, every corner tells a story of valor and artistry. Explore bustling Johari Bazaar for gemstones and textiles, dine like royalty in heritage havelis, and witness puppet shows and folk performances. Jaipur is the jewel of the Golden Triangle and the perfect introduction to Rajasthan's grandeur.

Must-See

  • Amber Fort (with elephant or jeep)
  • Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds)
  • City Palace & Jantar Mantar
  • Albert Hall Museum
  • Johari Bazaar & traditional markets

Best Time to Visit

  • Oct–Mar: Ideal weather for forts & palaces
  • Jan: Cool, clear skies, great for photography
  • Avoid: Apr–Jun (extreme heat)

Suggested Duration

  • 2–3 days: Key monuments + bazaars
  • 3–4 days: Add Nahargarh, block printing, cooking demo
  • Hub for Rajasthan circuit (Jodhpur, Udaipur)
