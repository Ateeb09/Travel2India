Delhi

The heart of India—where empires rose and fell, and history lives in every monument, bazaar, and bite.

India's capital is a dazzling collision of old and new. Ancient monuments like the Red Fort, Humayun's Tomb, and Qutub Minar stand alongside modern metro lines and bustling markets. Lose yourself in the lanes of Chandni Chowk for legendary street food, explore the grandeur of Mughal architecture, and feel the pulse of a city that has been the seat of power for centuries. Delhi is not just a destination—it's an immersion into India's soul.

Must-See

  • Red Fort & Chandni Chowk
  • Humayun's Tomb (UNESCO)
  • Qutub Minar complex
  • India Gate & Rajpath
  • Lotus Temple & Akshardham

Best Time to Visit

  • Oct–Mar: Pleasant, ideal for sightseeing
  • Oct–Nov: Post-monsoon greenery, festive season
  • Avoid: May–Jun (extreme heat), Dec–Jan (cold & fog)

Suggested Duration

  • 2–3 days: Major monuments + food tour
  • 4–5 days: Deep dive, museums, day trips to Agra/Jaipur
  • Perfect gateway to Golden Triangle
