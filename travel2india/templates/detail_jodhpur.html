Jodhpur

The Blue City—where Mehrangarh Fort watches over indigo lanes and royal heritage.

Jodhpur, the Blue City, spreads beneath the imposing Mehrangarh Fort—one of India's most impressive fortresses. The old city's blue-painted houses create a striking contrast against the desert landscape, while narrow lanes lead to spice markets, handicraft bazaars, and hidden temples. Visit the opulent Umaid Bhawan Palace, explore the fort's museums and palanquin galleries, and savor authentic Marwari cuisine. Jodhpur is Rajasthan at its most dramatic and atmospheric.

Must-See

  • Mehrangarh Fort (world-class museum)
  • Sardar Market & Clock Tower
  • Jaswant Thada (Marble Memorial)
  • Umaid Bhawan Palace
  • Blue city walk & rooftop views

Best Time to Visit

  • Oct–Mar: Pleasant desert climate
  • Oct: Marwar Festival (music & culture)
  • Avoid: Apr–Jun (very hot)

Suggested Duration

  • 2 days: Fort, markets, key sights
  • 2–3 days: Add Mandore, cooking class, village safari
  • Gateway to Jaisalmer & Thar Desert
