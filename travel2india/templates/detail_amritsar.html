Amritsar

Home of the Golden Temple—Sikhism's holiest shrine and India's warmest embrace.

Amritsar is defined by the Harmandir Sahib—the Golden Temple—a shimmering sanctuary in the middle of a sacred pool, open to all faiths 24/7. Experience the divine Langar, where tens of thousands are fed daily; witness the Wagah Border ceremony with its theatrical flag-lowering; and walk through Jallianwala Bagh, a moving memorial to India's freedom struggle. Amritsar is spiritual, historical, and unforgettable—a city that opens its heart to every visitor.

Must-See

  • Golden Temple (day & night visits)
  • Langar (community kitchen) experience
  • Wagah Border ceremony
  • Jallianwala Bagh Memorial
  • Partition Museum & old city bazaars

Best Time to Visit

  • Oct–Mar: Pleasant Punjab weather
  • Nov: Guru Nanak Jayanti (major Sikh festival)
  • Avoid: Apr–Jun (very hot), Dec–Jan (cold, fog)

Suggested Duration

  • 1–2 days: Golden Temple, Wagah, Jallianwala Bagh
  • 2 days: Add Partition Museum, food tour, evening aarti
  • Gateway to Punjab & Himachal
